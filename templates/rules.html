How to Play HoopMind

  • Guess the mystery NBA player in 6 tries or less.
  • After each guess, you'll see feedback for team, position, height, weight, conference, and division.
  • Green means your guess matches the mystery player's attribute. Red means it does not.
  • Up (↑) and down (↓) arrows for height and weight show if the mystery player is taller/heavier or shorter/lighter than your guess.
  • Use the dropdown menu to quickly find and select player names as you type.
  • Try to win with the fewest guesses possible. Good luck!
